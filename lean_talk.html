Some Links for the Lean Talk, 25 October 2025

URL of this page:
tinyurl.com/axlean

  • Where is Mathematics Going?, video of 2025 talk by Kevin Buzzard
  • The natural number game, a browser based gentle introduction to Lean 4 and the Peano axioms of the natural numbers
  • Watch someone play the number game; only go here if you get stuck; it's long
  • Continuous functions in Lean 4, video showing how to formalize the epsilon-delta criterion for continuous functions in Lean 4, and how to prove things about it. It is pretty fast paced and you won't understand everything right away.
  • You can install Lean 4 on your own machine, along with the free VS Code editor, or you can use Lean online, in your browser:
    • Installing Lean 4 and VS Code on your machine
    • Live Lean 4 online, running in the web browser
  • A Glimpse of Lean, a tutorial that gives a fairly short introduction to the most important aspects of Lean 4. You can follow along either with your own local Lean installation of with Live Lean 4 online
  • Mathematics in Lean. This is a book that explains it all slowly and systematically. It's best to use it with a local installation of Lean and VS Code, so that you can play with the examples and do the exercises.
